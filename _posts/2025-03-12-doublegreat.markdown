[doublegreat.dev](https://doublegreat.dev) Jekyll theme.

## Installation

Add this line to your Jekyll site's `_config.yml`:

```yaml
remote_theme: double-great/theme@main
```

And add to the `plugins` list:

```yaml
- jekyll-remote-theme
```
