Webcomic Jekyll Theme, a simple webcomic theme powered by Bootstrap.

This theme is meant to offer a free and relatively easy webcomic hosting solution for artists with little or no coding experience.

## Features

* Pagination
* Mobile-friendly
* Archive
* RSS feed
